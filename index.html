FANS NFTs



Fans NFTs will also play a vital role in the game, where
NFT holders benefit from a portion of the daily rewards
paid to players.

Simply use your Fan NFT and your SFP tokens to pay for
a game ticket and once the game is over, the rewards are
generated. Fans can pre register to as many games as
they wish to participate in, maximum number of fans per
game is capped at 100 fans.

  Fees Per Game: 1 SFP TOKEN

  Rewards: 20%
